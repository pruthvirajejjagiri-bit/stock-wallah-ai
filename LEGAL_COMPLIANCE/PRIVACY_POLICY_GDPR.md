### 5.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to:

**How to exercise:**
- Update in-app under Settings > Profile
- Or email: privacy@stockwallah.ai
- Changes take effect within 24 hours

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Have your account permanently removed

**Important notes:**

- Cannot be reversed once deleted
- Processing time: 30 days

**How to exercise:**
- Email: privacy@stockwallah.ai
- Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
- Include: Your account email and reason

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to:
- Receive your data in machine-readable format

**Included data:**
- Personal profile information

**Format:** CSV, JSON, or PDF

**How to exercise:**
- In-app: Settings > Data Export > Download
- Or email: privacy@stockwallah.ai

### 5.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to:
- Restrict how we use your data
- Limit data processing

**How to exercise:**
- Email: privacy@stockwallah.ai

### 5.6 Right to Object

You have the right to:
- Object to promotional communications
- Opt-out of analytics

**How to exercise:**

### 5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for:

## 6. DATA SECURITY

### 6.1 Security Measures

We implement:
- **Encryption in Transit:** HTTPS/TLS (256-bit)
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256-GCM
- **Authentication:** OAuth 2.0 + JWT tokens
- **Rate Limiting:** Prevent brute force attacks
- **Firewalls:** Network-level protection
- **Access Control:** Principle of least privilege
- **Audit Logging:** All sensitive actions logged
- **Intrusion Detection:** Monitor for attacks
- **Penetration Testing:** Regular security audits
- **Patch Management:** Keep systems updated

### 6.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for:
- **Active Users:** As long as account exists
- **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days (then permanent deletion)
- **Legal/Tax:** 7 years (for regulatory compliance)
- **Analytics:** Aggregated, anonymized (indefinite)

### 6.3 Data Breach Notification

If a breach occurs:
- We will notify you within 72 hours
- Notification via email and in-app
- We will explain what happened
- We will explain remediation steps
- We will comply with all legal requirements
